About

Christina Young Kim is an artist and designer. She creates visual art and print media by researching Contemporary art, philosophy, and innovative solutions. Her recent research in the Bachelor of Fine Arts in Visual Arts at Emily Carr University of Art + Design is in print media and interaction design. She has had the opportunity to exhibit in galleries and build a dedication to the arts. Her recent interest centers around self-expression, mixed media, and experimentation in the studio space.
