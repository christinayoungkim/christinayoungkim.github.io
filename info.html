About

Christina Young Kim is an artist and designer. She creates visual art and print media by researching new tools, trends, and innovative solutions. Her recent research in the Bachelor of Fine Arts in Visual Arts at Emily Carr University of Art + Design are in print media and interaction design. She has had the opportunity to exhibit in galleries and build a dedication towards the arts. Her recent interest centers around working in a studio space and creating cultural work and exhibition design.
